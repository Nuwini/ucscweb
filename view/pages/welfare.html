  • WIFI & Internet

    The students must submit the completed filled Wi-Fi connection request form to NOC room during office hours.The request forms can be downloaded from UCSC web site (ucsc.lk/noc).Only one device per student is allowed and service will be bind to a physical address of the device.The service will be activated after one week after submission of your completed application form.

  • Canteen

    The UCSC Canteen which is located inside the UCSC building complex,is open for students during the academic semesters from 7.00a.m. to 6.00 p.m.Canteens of other faculties are also available for the use of UCSC Students.

  • Health Services

    The Health Centre is located in a close proximity to the UCSC and these facilities are available for all UCSC students.It provides outpatient treatment and medical consulation for all health issues.A Dental Unit is also attached in this centre and it is open on Monday,Wednesday and Friday.The health service is organized to assist students to stay active and healthy life during their academic life.
