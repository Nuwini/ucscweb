  • Masters Degrees

    UCSC conducts Masters Degrees in Computer Science (MCS),Information Technology(MIT) and Information Security(MIS).MCS is a specialization degree for Computer Science and IT degree holders whereas MIT is a postgraduate degree for gradutes in non computing disciplines.MIT has two sub programmes allowing candidates to specialize in multimeda or e learning streams.MIS offers mid career opportunities for those working in the areas of Information Technology,Information System Audit and Information Security.

    View This Degree »

  • Reserach Degrees - MPhil Programme

    UCSC has several active research groups including wireless networks,distributed systems,natural languages,cryptographic systems,graphics and image processing,GIS databases,elearning and allowing capable researchers to work on appropriate and cutting edge technologies.Most of the research groups funded by foreign agencies,industry,National Science Foundation(NSF) and National Research Council(NRC).Those who have honors degree or masters degree could apply for research degrees at any time.

    View This Programme »

  • Research Degrees PhD Programme

    The PhD is the highest degree awarded by the University of Colombo School of Computing.Doctoral research involves working closely with faculty on projects of mutual interest.The School of Computing provides an excellent environment in which to pursue dictoral studies.Those who are register for MPhil,provisions are available to upgrade to a PhD candidature based on progress made during MPhil registration period.Faculty conduct research on diferent topics in computing.
